Our services

Strategy

Custom Marketing Plan

Before spending a dollar on ads or posting a single piece of content, you need a plan. Most companies skip this — and waste money as a result. We start every engagement by building a documented marketing strategy from scratch, tailored to your business, your audience, and your competitive landscape.

  • Full audit of your current online presence
  • Competitor analysis and gap identification
  • Target audience research and persona building
  • Channel selection: where to show up and why
  • 90-day roadmap with measurable KPIs
✦ Typical agencies charge $3,000–$10,000 for a strategy alone. At Promovix it's included in every plan.
Social Media

Social Media Management

Inconsistent social media is worse than no social media — it signals a company nobody is home. We manage your presence end-to-end: strategy, content creation, scheduling, engagement, and monthly reporting. You focus on your business. We make sure your brand is always active and always professional.

  • Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok management
  • On-brand content created and posted weekly
  • Community management and comment responses
  • Follower growth strategy and hashtag research
  • Monthly analytics report in plain language
✦ Market average for social media management: $1,000–$7,000/mo. Promovix starts at $500/mo.
SEO

Search Engine Optimization

SEO is the only marketing channel that keeps working after you stop paying. When someone searches for what you sell, you want to appear on page one of Google — not page five. We handle the technical structure, the content, and the keyword strategy that moves you up the rankings over time.

  • Keyword research targeting your ideal customers
  • On-page optimization (titles, structure, content)
  • Technical SEO: speed, mobile, indexing
  • Monthly rank tracking and competitor monitoring
  • Google Search Console setup and management
✦ SEO agencies charge $500–$10,000/mo. Our Growth plan includes SEO from $900/mo bundled with other services.
Content

Content Creation

Content is how you build trust online before a potential customer ever speaks to you. Blog posts rank on Google. Captions build brand personality. Newsletters keep customers coming back. Ad copy drives conversions. We write all of it — in your voice, for your audience, in any language.

  • SEO-optimized blog posts and website copy
  • Social media captions and creative concepts
  • Email newsletter copy that people actually read
  • Ad copy for Google, Meta, and LinkedIn
  • Available in English, Spanish, and Portuguese
✦ Content marketing agencies charge $2,000–$10,000/mo. Ours is bundled into plans starting at $900/mo.
AI Tools

AI-Assisted Marketing

AI has changed what a small marketing team can produce. We use AI tools across content creation, campaign testing, and performance analysis — which means we deliver the output of an agency 3x our size at a fraction of the cost. Every client benefits from this, not just enterprise clients.

  • AI-generated content that matches your brand voice
  • Automated A/B testing of ads and subject lines
  • Predictive campaign optimization
  • AI-assisted performance reporting and insights
  • 20–35% more content output vs. traditional agencies
✦ Agencies charge $2,000–$8,000/mo for AI-assisted campaigns. At Promovix it's standard in all plans.
Email

Email Marketing Campaigns

Email is still the highest-ROI channel in digital marketing — $36 back for every $1 spent on average. But only when it's done right: segmented, personalized, automated, and consistently optimized. We handle every part of your email marketing so your audience stays engaged and your list converts to revenue.

  • Segmented email list strategy and setup
  • Welcome sequences and nurture flows
  • Monthly newsletter creation and deployment
  • Open rate and click-through optimization
  • Full reporting: opens, clicks, revenue attribution
✦ Email marketing agencies charge $300–$5,000/mo. Ours is included in Growth and Professional plans.
